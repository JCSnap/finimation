# ETF Creation / Redemption Loop

Authorized participants (APs) arbitrage ETF premium/discount gaps.

## Premium case

If ETF price > NAV enough to cover frictions:

1. AP buys underlying basket
2. Delivers basket to issuer for ETF shares
3. Sells ETF shares in the market

## Discount case

If ETF price < NAV enough to cover frictions:

1. AP buys ETF shares
2. Redeems for underlying basket
3. Sells underlying basket

Net edge must exceed fees and execution costs.
